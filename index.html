Amik Japansrood is an artist that in my opinion deserved a better digital stage to show her work.

ABOUT THIS PROJECT...

Amik Goes Digital


What did the customer want?

Amik had a Wordpress-site that was only working on desktop. The mobile version was very broken and there was no contact page, so people didn't know where to find her. The theme she used was to be maintained, so there was not much to be changed. The items for sale had to be the first things that were visible, supplemented with all the rest of her work from the past, serving as her portfolio.

How did we achieve this goal?

The first thing i did was change the theme to a black theme which made her work stand out more. I changed the fonts to a more street-like font to match her authentic style. The next mission was to add a gallery view, as the site was there were only preview and a bad full-screen page when the user clicked the preview. The mobile version was implemented first, with a lazy load blabla the site was being made as light as possible, the images optimized to the maximum view, this was necessary as there were a lot of images to be displayed. The most fun part was the menu, as it was to be a challenge to my own designing skills. I managed to mix my love for geometric into the menu because this is what we can also find back in a lot of her work blabla.

What languages were used?

PHP, MySQL, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript & jQuery
