About me


I am a Webdesigner living in the Netherlands. I've been having a good time creating websites since 2014. Started with a Professional Webdesigner - education (LOI) and practicing with Codecademy I got into building my first websites. Since then I've been doing a lot of projects for free to build a portfolio and during my first job as webdesigner (2 years) I managed to develop myself into a medior webdesigner/developer and administrator, but as it goes with all good things, this will soon come to an end. Right now I'm looking around to see what else is out there, looking for the offer that best suits me.

Feel free to check out anything on my site to get to know me a bit more.

Goals & Objectives


(Personal) Goals


The main goal: a job at a web design-bureau. I like to be surrounded by people with the same interests and knowledge to exchange. I like the idea of designers and developers focussing at their own specialisms, as a freelancer I am used to do both and it's hard to switch your state of mind sometimes. Also, even though they ain't all that bad, i really like "to improve" my organization & planning-skills.



Objectives


As I have currently progressed a lot with the languages I wanted to learn I am now focussing on simplifying and organizing my code with BEM (blocks/elements/modifiers) and Sass (Syntactically Awesome Style Sheets). Last but not least I am planning to learn more pure Javascript, to both speed up the script without using jQuery as well as to be ready to learn frameworks like Angular and React.
